About FIDV

The mission of FIDV-Beit Halochem (House of the Warriors) is to care for Israeli servicemen and women wounded in the line of duty who are now disabled. At our state-of-the-art rehabilitation centers in Tel Aviv, Haifa, Jerusalem and Beer Sheva we serve more than 51,000 disabled veterans and family members. We raise awareness and funds for a broad range of social, cultural and sports training programs, offer scholarships, develop new initiatives, and provide specialized equipment for the centers. We help empower them to rebuild their lives.
