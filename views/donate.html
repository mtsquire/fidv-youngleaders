Donate to FIDV

Though it's been over one year since Operation Protective Edge, the lives of thousands of soldiers wounded in battle have changed forever. They have been seriously injured, and now they face a daily struggle with the difficult rehabilitation process, which for many will be a life-long battle.

Please join us as we honor these brave soldiers on Saturday, March 10th at The Lighthouse at Chelsea Piers, for the 4nd Annual Winter Gala presented by the FIDV Young Leadership Board. It will be a night to celebrate life, while also honoring the sacrifices made by the brave soldiers – young and old – who have defended the homeland of Israel for us all.

At our Beit Halochem rehabilitation centers in Jerusalem, Tel Aviv, Haifa, and Be’er Sheva, we provide a broad range of personalized services and therapies for more than 51,000 disabled veterans and terror victims. The Beit Halochem centers offer a healing community of those who have "been there." They help each other meet and overcome the challenges of disability, on the social, physical and psychological levels. FIDV-Beit Halochem does it all!

About FIDV

The men and women of the Israel Defense Forces serve their country with courage. The debt of gratitude the Jewish world owes them is inestimable. As well, we owe much to the courageous Israelis who, by living in Israel, secure this tiny land for all Jews.

Friends of Israel Disabled Veterans (FIDV) and Beit Halochem are committed to caring for Israeli servicemen and women disabled in the line of duty. Beit Halochem centers provide therapeutic, sports and recreational facilities to help them resume lives of purpose and dignity.
